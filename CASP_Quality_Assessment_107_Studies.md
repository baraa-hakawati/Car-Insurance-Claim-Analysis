## METHODOLOGY NOTES

**CASP Framework Applied:**
This assessment utilized the Critical Appraisal Skills Programme (CASP) framework adapted for information systems and CRM research. Each study was evaluated across 8-9 criteria depending on study type (empirical vs. conceptual).

**Scoring Approach:**
- Conservative scoring applied when full methodological details unavailable
- "Not assessable from available information" marked as score of 1 (not 0, not 2)
- No methodological details invented or assumed
- Confidence flags clearly indicate assessment limitations

**Key Considerations:**
1. Many studies lacked full text access, limiting detailed methodological assessment
2. Conference papers and book chapters scored lower on replicability criteria
3. Systematic reviews and meta-analyses scored highly on theoretical grounding
4. Industry reports and white papers assessed differently than peer-reviewed research

**Assessment Completed:** January 31, 2026
